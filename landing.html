About Sahayak

Our Mission

To empower teachers with AI tools that reduce their workload while enhancing the learning experience for students across different grade levels and learning abilities.

How It Works

Sahayak leverages Google's Gemini AI models to understand teacher needs and generate relevant educational content. The platform works even with limited internet connectivity and supports multiple Indian languages.

For Teachers, By Teachers

Our development team has worked closely with educators from rural schools across India to ensure Sahayak addresses real classroom challenges and is intuitive to use, even for those with limited technology experience.

Offline Capability

Sahayak works seamlessly even without internet access. Download materials when connected and access them anytime, anywhere - perfect for schools with unreliable internet connectivity.
